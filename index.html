Hi, I’m Ernesto Ramirez.

I’m the Director of R&D at Fitabase, where I’m helping researchers take advantage of the full potential offered by consumer mhealth tools. I’m a public health researcher by training, but have a keen interest in understanding how personal data can be used for public good. Here, you’ll find some thoughs, tools, and tips on data, research, and whatever else I’m currently working on.

What am I up to right now?

Since late 2015 I’ve been fiddling around with R. By no means am I an expert, but I like exploring it’s depth and learning how to apply new methods to data I work with and/or find interesting. Check out my R miscellenea by exploring /categories/R.
